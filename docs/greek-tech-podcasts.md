## 42 Minutes of SaaS

Listen to 42 minutes of SaaS on Spotify. Two engineers exploring the world of building a bootstrapped SaaS business.

---

## Μικρή Κουβέντα (Mikri Kouventa)

[**Μικρή Κουβέντα**](https://mikrikouventa.fm/) (*Mikri Kouventa*) is a **video podcast** in Greek about technology, entrepreneurship, and current affairs. The project also publishes **Small Talk**, an English-language series with similar themes.

---

## Hustle Hours

[**Hustle Hours**](https://www.hustlehours.com/) started as a podcast on youth entrepreneurship and grew into a **community for founders**: interviews, Startup School-style videos, and **events** connecting builders in Greece (and further afield).

---

## Outliers

[**Outliers**](https://outliers.gr/el/) by **Endeavor Greece** spotlights standout founders: weekly conversations with people behind successful startups in Greece and abroad—journeys, lessons, and sector outlooks—with host **Panagiotis Karabinis** (CEO, Endeavor Greece). Episodes and notes also live on [outliers.gr](https://outliers.gr/el/).

---

## SmartTalks

[**SmartTalks**](https://www.smarttalks.gr) from **[Infocom.gr](https://www.infocom.gr/)** and **Smartpress**—interviews on **technology, telecoms, and business**, hosted by journalist **Christos Kotsakas** (*Χρήστος Κοτσακάς*). Episodes are listed on [smarttalks.gr](https://www.smarttalks.gr).

---

## Hack U, με τον Μίλτο Μακρίδη

[**Hack U, με τον Μίλτο Μακρίδη**](https://pod.gr/hack-u/) (*Hack U*) from **[pod.gr](https://pod.gr/)** explores how **digital culture and innovation** shape everyday life—AI, blockchain, and more—with host **Μίλτος Μακρίδης** (*Miltos Makridis*) and Greek researchers, founders, and makers.

---

## AUEB Cast

[**AUEB Cast**](https://aueb-cast.simplecast.com/) is the **Athens University of Economics and Business** podcast: university life, news, and **science and business** topics (often in English)—from the school’s wider channel on [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLLdBJrqWUK9oxmJ-ThUB0XUU6m6H4qBGQ).

---

## Innovative Greeks Talks

[**Innovative Greeks Talks**](https://innovative-greeks-talks.simplecast.com/) is a **monthly** interview show with high-achieving Greeks abroad and at home—hosted by **Μάρκος Βερέμης** (*Markos Veremis*), chair of the **ΣΕΒ** innovation committee—produced with partners including **Eurobank** and **Grant Thornton** (see also the **[Innovative Greeks](https://www.youtube.com/@InnovativeGreeks)** YouTube presence).

---

## Innovation Talks (Greece)

[**Innovation Talks (Greece)**](https://innovationtalks.gr/) from **Crowdpolicy** covers **innovation, entrepreneurship, and emerging technologies** in Greek—conversations with colleagues and guests across the local ecosystem.

---

## Tech Leadership Podcast

[**Tech Leadership Podcast**](https://mariosfakiolas.com/podcast/)—weekly episodes on **technology through leadership and management**, with host **Μάριος Φακιόλας** (*Marios Fakiolas*). **[Podlist.gr](https://podlist.gr/p/tech-leadership-podcast-410)** lists **Apple Podcasts** and **Spotify** alongside the site.

## Deploy Να Τελειώνουμε

Ενα video podcast που θα μιλάμε για cloud, υποδομή και ό,τι άλλο ενδιαφέρoν απο τη καθημερινότητά μας!!
