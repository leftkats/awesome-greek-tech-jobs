Greek tech & startup podcasts

Hand-picked video and audio shows from Greece that often cover technology, startups, product, and engineering—useful listening next to the employer directory and job-search links in search-queries-and-resources.md.

Μικρή Κουβέντα (Mikri Kouventa)

Μικρή Κουβέντα (Mikri Kouventa) is a video podcast in Greek about technology, entrepreneurship, and current affairs. The project also publishes Small Talk, an English-language series with similar themes.
Site mikrikouventa.fm
YouTube @mikrikouventa
Apple Podcasts Μικρή Κουβέντα (Greece)
Spotify Show

Hustle Hours

Hustle Hours started as a podcast on youth entrepreneurship and grew into a community for founders: interviews, Startup School-style videos, and events connecting builders in Greece (and further afield).
Site hustlehours.com
YouTube @HustleHoursPodcast
Spotify Hustle Hours on Spotify
